遇见三维

三维视觉是人类感知世界的重要手段之一。我们生活在一个三维的世界中，通过视觉系统捕捉深度、形状和空间关系，从而理解周围的环境。三维视觉技术不仅是计算机视觉领域的核心研究方向，更是连接虚拟与现实、推动智能感知发展的关键技术。遇见三维致力于探索和分享三维视觉的前沿技术与应用，让更多人了解这个充满魅力的领域。

核心成员

颜青松毕业于武汉大学测绘学院，分别于2016年、2019年、2024年获得武汉大学的学士学位、硕士学位和博士学位。他的主要研究方向包括全景视觉、三维重建、多视图立体匹配、神经辐射场、3D Gaussian Splatting等。目前在XGRIDS担任三维重建工程师。

任浩凡在杭州电子科技大学IIPL实验室获得学士和硕士学位，师从杭州电子科技大学的严承刚教授和英特尔中国研究院的卢明研究员。目前在XGRIDS担任工程师，主要负责三维重建工作，特别是与3D Gaussian Splatting相关的研究与开发。

Yujian3D

3D vision is one of the essential means by which humans perceive the world. We live in a three-dimensional world, capturing depth, shape, and spatial relationships through our visual system to understand our surroundings. 3D vision technology is not only a core research direction in computer vision but also a key technology connecting the virtual and real worlds, driving the development of intelligent perception. Yujian3D is dedicated to exploring and sharing cutting-edge technologies and applications in 3D vision, helping more people understand this fascinating field.

Core Members

Qingsong Yan graduated from the School of Geodesy and Geomatics at Wuhan University. He obtained his Bachelor’s degree in 2016, Master’s degree in 2019, and Ph.D. degree in 2024 from Wuhan University. His main research interests include panorama vision, 3D reconstruction, multi-view stereo, neural radiance fields, and 3D Gaussian Splatting. He is currently working as a 3D Reconstruction Engineer at XGRIDS.

Haofan Ren received his bachelor’s and master’s degrees from IIPL Lab of Hangzhou Dianzi University, supervised by Prof. Chenggang Yan from HDU and Ming Lu from Intel Labs China. He is currently an engineer at XGRIDS, mainly responsible for 3D reconstruction, especially work related to 3D Gaussian Splatting.
